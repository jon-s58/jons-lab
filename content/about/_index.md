Jon's Lab is a modern polymath's workshop — where software, hardware, and ideas are forged from first principles.

Part inventor's lab, part blacksmith's forge, part philosopher's study. Every product carries the maker's mark of someone who builds across disciplines: from code to metal, from deep learning to woodcraft.

## Philosophy

The best solutions come from thinking across boundaries. A firmware problem might yield to a woodworker's intuition about grain direction. A machine learning architecture might mirror the way a blacksmith reads heat in steel. When you build with your hands and your mind in equal measure, you develop a sense for what's *right* — not just what's correct.

## What We Build

We take on projects that demand more than one discipline. The kind of work where knowing how metal behaves under stress makes you a better systems programmer, and understanding distributed systems makes you a better inventor.

- **Software** — Systems programming, web platforms, developer tools
- **Hardware** — Embedded systems, custom electronics, physical computing
- **Deep Learning** — Applied ML, computer vision, neural architecture
- **Physical Craft** — Metalwork, woodcraft, precision fabrication
